About Durra

The name "Durra" is inspired by the Arabic word (ÿØÿ±ÿ©) meaning "pearl" or things of value. Pearls diving and craftsmanship dives long in Bahrain's history and symbolizes value, inspired by such, Durra has been developed through meticulous market analysis, user-centric design, and rigorous testing to deliver a refined marketplace experience.

Durra is a jewelry digital marketplace developed to connect jewelers and buyers in Bahrain, increasing the visibility of local jewelers and facilitating the shopping process for buyers.

Abstract

Durra is a jewelry digital marketplace developed to connect jewelers and buyers in Bahrain, increasing the visibility of local jewelers and facilitating the shopping process for buyers. The platform is the result of careful market analysis and requirement elicitation. Upon the findings obtained, a user-centric prototype was developed to ensure a positive usability experience for users. The design was then implemented into an interactive platform that went through an iteration of testing and refinement phases to measure users' acceptance and act on their feedback. Future plans revolve around the development of a re-selling feature, real-time tracking of orders feature, and a mobile platform. Durra does also aim to integrate with more authentication and certifications providers. To conclude, all plans are to contribute to the improvement of Durra's usability and trustworthiness as envisioned by its users.

Project Objectives

01

To develop Durra, a dedicated digital marketplace that connects Bahraini jewelers directly with customers, enhancing the local jewelry industry's digital presence.

02

To significantly increase the visibility and market reach of Bahraini jewelers, both locally and internationally, by providing a centralized, trusted online platform.

03

To streamline and improve the customer journey by simplifying the process of searching for, comparing, and discovering fine jewelry pieces from multiple vendors in one place.

04

To ensure customers have access to concurrent, fair market prices and can easily compare options, fostering trust and informed decision-making.

05

To guarantee the authenticity of jewelry by leveraging and integrating official gemological authentication services, notably from DANAT (Bahrain's Institute for Pearls and Gemstones).

06

To create a secure, reliable ecosystem that benefits both jewelers and customers by offering a convenient, verified, and superior shopping experience.

Methodology & Approach

Durra was developed using an Agile methodology, an iterative approach that emphasizes flexibility, continuous improvement, and user feedback. Agile enables us to rapidly prototype, test, and refine the platform based on real user insights and market demands.

Development Process

01

Market Research & User Requirements Collection

In-depth analysis of the jewelry market in Bahrain and extensive user interviews

02

Design of User-centric Prototypes

Creation of intuitive wireframes and interactive prototypes with continuous user feedback

03

MERN Stack Implementation

Development using MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js for robust functionality

04

Continuous Testing & Quality Assurance

Rigorous manual testing and iterative refinement based on user feedback
